Construction is music and other things by Nadya Peek and Marko Ahtisaari. We released the EPs WE'RE GREAT THANKS FOR ASKING (2020) and SET (2018), which you should preferably listen to on a record player. We have remixes by cool friends on RE:SET (2019). Otherwise, we have a newsletter and videos, and we're on bandcamp, soundcloud, spotify, instagram, and twitter. Thanks for listening.
